Privacy Policy

Ssak: Three Habits · Effective: April 2, 2026

Ssak: Three Habits does not operate any server. All data is stored only on your device. We do not collect any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or sign-in is required.
  • We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personally identifying information.
  • We do not collect location information.
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.

2. Data Storage

Habit names, check-in records, settings, and any other data you enter are stored only in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage).

  • Your data is never transmitted to any external server.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data from your device.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not provide, share, or sell any data to third parties.

4. Notifications

If you enable habit reminders, notifications are delivered through your device's local notification system. Reminder settings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted externally.

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, this policy applies equally regardless of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the changes on this page and update the effective date. For material changes, we may also notify you within the app.
